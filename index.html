About

        What's re/tell? (some of you who may wonder) Re/tell is a re-imagining or telling the story/ events through a different perspective that may not be true to the roots of realms. To be specific, the scenario can be based on either a build's feature, myths, monster, or anything realm related. Even the mechanics can be altered depending on the designated genre. Also the /tell itself is play on words with the chat command scheme. Mostly the format of the tale consist on the structure sometimes similar to a interactive fiction or should I say comic style. If you happen to dislike this sort of direction, then I suggest you to look elsewhere if it doesn't intrigued your interest. This is the first time I'll attempt this method through thread form. (Experimental) Thank you for your patience.
